# Multi-Page Sales Dashboard  
### Business Sales Performance Analysis using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project features a **multi-page interactive Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze business performance across different dimensions.

The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand:
- Sales and profit trends
- Segment-wise performance
- Country and product contribution
- Discount and profitability relationships
- Seasonal sales behavior

Each page focuses on a specific analytical view to support better decision-making.

  High-level KPIs for sales, profit, discounts, and units sold

  Performance comparison across business segments

- **Country & Product Analysis**  
  Geographic and product-level contribution to profit

- **Discount & Profit Analysis**  
  Impact of discounting strategies on profitability

---

## Key Business Questions Explored

- How do sales and profit vary over time?
- Which segments and countries drive the most profit?
- Do higher discounts lead to lower profitability?
- Which products contribute most to overall performance?
- Are there seasonal trends in sales and profit?

---

## Key Insights (High-Level)

- Certain segments consistently outperform others in profitability
- High sales volumes do not always result in higher profit
- Excessive discounting negatively impacts profit margins
- Clear seasonality is visible across sales trends

*(Insights are descriptive and intended for business analysis.)*
